1. **Use the repository itself as the source of truth.**
2. **Inspect all relevant files, not just the README.**

4. **Do not give me fluffy best practices without pointing to concrete repository evidence.**

   - Missing evidence / blind spots
6. **Be blunt and opinionated.**
7. **Do not stop at "looks fine".**
