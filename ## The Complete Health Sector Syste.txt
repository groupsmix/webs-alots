## MASTER RULE FIRST

Every system has **5 user levels:**

```
1. Super Admin (YOU)     → control everything across all clients
2. Clinic Admin          → the doctor or clinic owner
3. Receptionist          → manages bookings, patients daily
4. Doctor                → sees his patients only
5. Patient               → books, sees history, uploads files
```

Never forget the **Receptionist role.** Most agencies do. Every clinic has one and she runs everything.
